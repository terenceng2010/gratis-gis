fix(web-map): use rasterized alpha directly as SDF source 
 
The runtime chamfer-distance transform was producing images that 
MapLibre either rejected or rendered invisibly - so picking an 
icon made the feature disappear entirely. Swap to passing the 
plain rasterized icon straight to addImage with sdf: true; 
MapLibre treats the alpha channel as a coarse distance field and 
tinting via icon-color works. Edges are slightly harder than 
what a proper per-pixel SDF would produce, but the feature is 
always visible, which is the bigger win. 
 
The sdf.ts helper stays in the tree so a future SDF-backed 
upgrade (precomputed atlases, tiny-sdf, etc.) has the seam to 
plug into.
